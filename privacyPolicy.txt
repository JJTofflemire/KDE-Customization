Privacy Policy for Personal Use of Aerc Token Generator Script

Effective Date: 2023-11-30

This privacy policy describes how Aerc Token Generator Script (the "App") accesses and uses your personal information when you use it with your Gmail account.

Information the App Accesses

The App uses Google OAuth to access your Gmail account. This means the App needs permission to:

View your email address and profile picture. This is necessary for authentication and to display your information within the app.
View, send, and receive emails. This is the core functionality of any email client, allowing you to manage your emails within the app.
How the App Uses Your Information

The App uses your information solely to provide you with email client functionality. This includes:

Displaying your emails.
Allowing you to compose, send, receive, and manage emails.
Providing a user interface that reflects your account information.
